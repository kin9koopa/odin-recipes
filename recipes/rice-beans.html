Caribbean Rice and Beans

yield:6-8 Servings

prep time: 20 MINUTES

cook time: 25 MINUTES

additional time: 20 MINUTES

total time: 1 HOUR 5 MINUTES

Rice cooked in coconut milk with beans and spicy pepper, Caribbean rice and beans is on the Caribbean slope of Costa Rica

*****

4.8 Stars (4 Reviews)

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1.5 cups coconut milk, NOT low fat version
  • 2 cups long grain white rice rinsed and drained
  • 1 whole Panamanian pepper can substitute scotch bonnet or habanero pepper
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1.5 cups cooked red beans about 1/2 cup dried

instructions

  1. In a heavy bottomed stock pot, add water and coconut milk. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Once boiling add rice, panamanian pepper and thyme. Bring to a simmer and cover until cooked.
  3. Remove pepper. Add in cooked beans and season to taste.
